As a Master Mason, you've begun a profound journey. Royal Arch Masonry reveals deeper truths, preserved through generations. It is the capstone of Ancient Craft Masonry, the missing word, the fuller story of who we are and why it matters.

Our Pillars

Brotherhood

Fraternity is the foundation. Our Chapter thrives through shared ritual, mutual trust, and a commitment to each other as Companions and Masons.

Purpose

Royal Arch Masonry invites us to seek what was lost, recovering ancient truths and carrying them forward in our lives and Lodges.

Legacy

The Royal Arch degrees challenge us to reflect on the meaning of our Masonic heritage, and to preserve and pass on the Light for generations to come.
